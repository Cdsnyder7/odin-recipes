Creamy Homemade Macoroni & Cheese

This recipe will teach you how to prepare a creamy, rich Mac & Cheese that your family won't get enough of. We will not be making the pasta from scratch as that is a whole other ballgame. I hope you enjoy!

  • 1 pound of dried shell pasta
  • 1 cup of flower
  • 1 stick of unsalted butter
  • 6 cups of milk
  • 2 Cups of aged sharp chedder
  • 1/4 cup of green onions
  • Add what ever mix ins you please
  1. Place a medieum sized pot on med/hi heat and melt butter. After the butter is melted slowly stir in the cup of flour
  2. After about three minutes, pour warm milk over the light brown rue about 1 cup at a time. If it gets thin add flour and keep mixing.
  3. Continue to mix for about 3 mintues then add your cheese and whatever other mix ins you like.
