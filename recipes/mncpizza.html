Mac N Cheese Pizza

Macoroni & Cheese Pizza

The cheesiest of Pizzas

This recipe will help you to unlock the golden delicacy that is mac n cheese pizza. This dinner is one known to satisfy any dairy desire you could ever have. You can make this from scratch with the mac n cheese recipe you find on the home page, but the quick and easy version is as follows.

Ingrediants

  • Frozen Cheese Pizza
  • Cracker barrel boxed Mac & Cheese (if you are going to do a boxed mac n cheese this is the only way to go)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to the temperature on the box for the pizza. Cook the pizza for the alloted time on the box.
  2. When the pizza has about 15-20 minutes left, start boiling the water for the mac n cheese.
  3. Throw the pasta in the boiling water and cook for 9-10 minutes. Then stir in that processed golen liquid.
  4. When the pizza has 5 minutes left, pull it out, top it with the mac n cheese and (if you want even more dair) top it with some shredded cheese to help hold the macaroni to the pizza.
  5. Enjoy the only meal you will ever think about for the rest of your life.
