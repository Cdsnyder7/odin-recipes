Sweet Potato Pasta

Underrated Meal of the Month

This is a simple recipe that will suprise you with its subtle delicousness. If you want to make it even simpler buy already cubed sweet potatos.

  • 1 Pound of Pasta
  • 1 pound of diced sweet potatos
  • 4 tablespoons of oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 2 Tsp of red chili flakes
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic
  • Whatever herb you would like to top it with
  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees
  2. Place the cubed sweet potatos into a large, rimmed baking sheet and mix with 2 tablespoons of olive oil, salt and pepper. Bake in the oven for 15 minutes.
  3. After baking for 15 minutes, take the sweet potato out, toss in the garlic and mix well. Place the sweet potato back in for another 15 minutes. While it is roasting, start to boil the pasta.
  4. When the pasta is done, save 1 cup of cooked pasta water, and then drain.
  5. When the sweet potatos are done, pull them out and toss with the red chili flakes and your choice of herb. Drop the sweet potato into the pasta and then dribble another tablespoon of olive oil and mix. Stir in a little cooked pasta sauce, just enough to coat the pasta.
  6. Serve warm to happy bellies!
